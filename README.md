## FlatFinder
FlatFinder is a web-based application designed to help single, eligible HDB buyers find their dream, resale HDB flats in Singapore. FlatFinder allows users to filter flat listings based on their preferences such as preferred HDB type, estate and bank loan while also considering the eligible grants calculated from their financial information such as salary, CPF Ordinary Account balance, intended housing loan type and preferred price range.
